Why GRP

Stronger, lighter, longer-lasting.

  • Recommended

    GRP

    Lightest · longest-lasting

    Weight
    1×
    Life
    50+ yr
    Corrosion
    None
    Sections
    12 m

    Resin-rich liners resist corrosion; the structural glass wall carries the load. No cathodic protection, a 50-year service life, at a fraction of the weight of steel or concrete.

  • Ductile iron

    Heavy · protected

    Weight
    4×
    Life
    20–30 yr
    Corrosion
    Needs CP
    Sections
    6 m

    Heavy and corrosion-prone in soil — relies on linings and cathodic protection, with a shorter design life and install rigging several times heavier than GRP.

  • Concrete

    Heaviest

    Weight
    10×
    Life
    30–50 yr
    Corrosion
    Rebar rust
    Sections
    2.5–5 m

    Massive, brittle, steel-reinforced — long joint runs, high hauling weight, and vulnerability to cracking and chemical attack over a shorter, less predictable service life.
