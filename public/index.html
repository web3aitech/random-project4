Who we are

The only UAE-licensed Flowtite® producer.

Dubai Pipes Factory Co. manufactures Glass Reinforced Plastic pipe systems from a 150,000 m² plant in the Jebel Ali Industrial Area, inside Dubai Investments Park — around 100 km from Abu Dhabi.

Pipes are produced under Flowtite® technology and machinery, in continuous-filament and reciprocal helical winding, and are in use across the Middle East, the GCC and the wider UAE — for water, sewerage, irrigation and seawater networks.

Why GRP

Stronger, lighter, longer-lasting.

A fraction of the weight of steel or concrete — and built to outlast both. Hover a pipe to compare.

  • Recommended

    GRP

    Lightest · longest-lasting

    Weight
    1×
    Service life
    50+ yr
    Corrosion
    None
    Sections
    12 m

    A glass-reinforced plastic composite — resin-rich liners resist corrosion while the structural glass wall carries the load. No cathodic protection, a 50-year service life, at a fraction of the weight of steel or concrete.

  • Ductile iron

    Heavy · protected

    Weight
    4×
    Service life
    20–30 yr
    Corrosion
    Needs CP
    Sections
    6 m

    Strong in isolation but heavy and corrosion-prone in soil — it relies on linings and cathodic protection to survive buried service, with a shorter design life and install rigging several times heavier than GRP.

  • Concrete

    Heaviest

    Weight
    10×
    Service life
    30–50 yr
    Corrosion
    Rebar rust
    Sections
    2.5–5 m

    Massive, brittle and reinforced with steel that rusts — long joint runs, high hauling weight, and vulnerability to cracking and chemical attack over a shorter, less predictable service life.
