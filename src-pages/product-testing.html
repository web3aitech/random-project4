Product Testing

Quality procedure

From raw material to finished pipe

The quality procedure described here ensures the fulfillment of product testing and inspection as prescribed in the Quality Management System implemented at DPFC. Quality of raw material and manufactured products are controlled by:

  • Adherence to international standards and customer requirements
  • Performance of testing and inspection according to documented internal work instructions
  • Systematic collection of information at various stages from receipt of raw material to finished products

Inspection & Test Plan

The Inspection and Test Plan implemented by Dubai Pipes starts by inspecting incoming raw material, passes through process control and monitoring, and continues to finished-product testing. Testing can be destructive or non-destructive, dimensional and visual, conducted at predetermined frequency.

A standard inspection and test plan is available and implemented by Dubai Pipes; it meets the requirement for most international testing standards and may be modified based on customer requirements.

Qualification tests

In addition to regular online product and raw-material testing, the following long-term tests are carried out internally to qualify incoming raw material and predict the long-term performance of the produced pipes:

  • Strain Corrosion - ASTM D 3681: chemical resistance of fiberglass pipe in deflection condition
  • Hydrostatic Design Basis - ASTM D 2992: obtaining hydrostatic/pressure design basis for fiberglass pipe and fittings
  • Ring Bending - ASTM D 5365: long-term ring bending strain of fiberglass pipe

Material specifications

GRP pipes and fittings manufactured by Dubai Pipes Factory Co. are in accordance with the following international standards:

  • ASTM D3262 - Standard Specification for Fiberglass Sewer Pipe
  • ASTM D3517 - Standard Specification for Fiberglass Pressure Pipe
  • ASTM D3754 - Standard Specification for Fiberglass Sewer and Industrial Pressure Pipe
  • BS 5480 (superseded by) BS EN 14364 - GRP pipes, joints and fittings for water supply and sewerage
  • BS EN 1796 - GRP pipes, joints and fittings for drainage and sewerage
  • AWWA C950 - Fiberglass Pressure Pipe
