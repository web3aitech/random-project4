Certifications & Approvals

Quality committed

ISO & BSI Kitemark accredited

DPFC is quality-committed and ISO 9001:2008 accredited. A documented Quality Management System Manual confirms compliance with the ISO standard; the manual is available upon request.

Conformity with international standards was established by independent party BSI, and a satisfactory assessment of the manufacturing control process confirmed that such conformity is consistently achieved. Accordingly the following Kitemark licenses are obtained:

  • ASTM D 3262 - Kitemark License KM 505457
  • ASTM D 3517 - Kitemark License KM 505456
  • ASTM D 3754 - Kitemark License KM 505455
  • BS 5480 - Kitemark License KM 505454
  • BS EN 1796 - Kitemark License KM 549259
  • BS EN 14364 - Kitemark License KM 505454

DPFC products are approved by the Water Regulations Advisory Scheme (WRAS). DPFC is committed to the environmental management system framework and requirements of the ISO 14001:2004 standard.

Approvals

Products produced by Dubai Pipes factory are approved and in use across many of the Middle East countries, GCC countries, and specifically in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and the other Northern Emirates of the UAE. Approvals have been received from most of the local and international contractors, project consultants and governmental authorities. A large amount of pipe produced by Dubai Pipes is currently in use, mainly for sewerage, irrigation and storm-water networks.

Project record

Every GRP installation since July 2003

The DPFC project record is a running log of every GRP pipe supply the factory has delivered, kept in Dubai Municipality (DM) format. Each entry records the client, contractor and consultant, the project and location, the pipe specification — diameter (DN), pressure class (PN) and stiffness class (SN) — and the quantity supplied in metres.

It covers sewerage, irrigation and storm-water networks across Dubai, Abu Dhabi and the wider GCC, from DN 150 service connections up to DN 2400 trunk mains, with pressure ratings to PN 16 and stiffness classes up to SN 10000. Manhole liners and fittings are listed alongside the pipe quantities.
